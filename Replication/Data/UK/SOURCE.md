# United Kingdom — Capital Stock Source

## File
`a-millennium-of-macroeconomic-data-for-the-uk.xlsx`

## Original Source
Bank of England (2023). "A Millennium of Macroeconomic Data for the UK."
Thomas, R. and Dimsdale, N. (2017). Dataset v3.1.

Download: https://www.bankofengland.co.uk/statistics/research-datasets

## Sheet Used
`A55. Capital Stock`

## Column Used
- Row offset 7+, Column index 4 (0-based): Non-dwellings, market sector (£ millions, net)

## Variable Constructed
Net productive capital stock = Non-dwellings, market sector
(excludes residential dwellings and public sector capital)

## Period
Available from mid-19th century (we use 1870–1913)

## Notes
The sheet contains several capital stock concepts. We use column index 4 (NonDwellMktSec), the market-sector non-dwelling net capital stock, as the closest Marxian equivalent of productive capital deployed by private capitalist firms. This follows Feinstein & Pollard (1988) underlying estimates as compiled in the Bank of England dataset.

Column index 3 (NonDwellWE, whole economy) would include government capital; we exclude it to focus on private productive capital.
