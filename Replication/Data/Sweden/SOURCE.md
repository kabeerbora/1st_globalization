# Sweden — Capital Stock Source

## File
`tablesAtoX.xls`

## Original Source
Edvinsson, R. (2016). "New Annual Estimates of Swedish GDP, 1800–2010." *Economic History Review*, 69(4), 1102–1126.

Data available via: https://www.historicalstatistics.org

## Sheet Used
`Data`

## Columns Used
- Column 107 (index 106): Private sector net total capital stock (million SEK)
- Column 101 (index 100): Residential buildings, net (million SEK)

Note: The year column is at index 99 (column 100 in 1-based).

## Variable Constructed
Net productive private capital = Private net total − Residential buildings
(Col.107 − Col.101 in 1-based column numbering)

## Period
Available from 1800 (we use 1870–1913)

## Notes
Sweden's capital stock is already measured net of depreciation in the Edvinsson dataset. We subtract residential buildings to obtain the productive capital concept consistent with Marxian analysis (means of production, not dwellings). This series was confirmed to be approximately equivalent to the capital stock used in the original dataset for this paper.
